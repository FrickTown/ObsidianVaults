## Trig identities
$$\begin{split}
&sin(x) = cos(\frac\pi2 * x)\\
&sin(x)^2 + cos(x)^2\\
&sin(x ± y) = sin(x) * cos(y) ± cos(x) * sin(y)\\
&cos(x±y) = cos(x) * cos(y) ∓ sin(x) * sin(y)\\
&sin(2x) = 2*sin(x) * cos(x)\\
& cos(2x) = cos²(x) - sin²(x)\\

&\Rightarrow cos(2x) = 1 - 2*sin(x)
\end{split}$$

## Talföljder
#### Aritmetiska summan
$$\sum_{k=0}^{n}f(x) = \frac{f(0) + f(n)}{2}(1+n-k)$$
#### Geometriska summan
$$\sum_{k=0}^{n}f(x) = f(0)*\frac{1-r^{(1+n-k)}}{1-r}$$
